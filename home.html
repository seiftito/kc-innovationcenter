Imhotep I&D Center

Introduction

The Imhotep Innovation & Development Center in Knowledge City, Egypt’s New Administrative Capital, bridges the genius of the past with the possibilities of the future. Part of the national innovation ecosystem led by ITIDA and the EME initiative, it hosts startups and advanced labs, including Microelectronics, Digital Transformation, and Digital Fabrication. The center nurtures ideas, develops technologies, and empowers innovators to turn challenges into opportunities, strengthening Egypt as a regional hub for technology and electronics.
